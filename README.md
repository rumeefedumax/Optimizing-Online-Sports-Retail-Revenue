# Optimizing-Online-Sports-Retail-Revenue
Sports clothing and athleisure attire is a huge industry, worth approximately $193 billion in 2021 with a strong growth forecast over the next decade!  In this project, I play the role of a product analyst for an online sports clothing company. The company is specifically interested in how it can improve revenue. I dive into product data such as pricing, reviews, descriptions, and ratings, as well as revenue and website traffic, to produce recommendations for its marketing and sales teams.
